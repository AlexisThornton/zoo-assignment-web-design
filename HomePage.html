Melbourne Zoo

Melbourne Zoo is located in Melbourne, Australia. The zoo is open open from 9am to 8pm.
The zoo is included in Zoos Victoria which is dedicated to fighting wildlife extinction.

Use this map to see the animals!

Lemurs and Snow Leopards are two of the amazing animals that live in the Melbourne Zoo!

Zoos Victoria is commited to fighting extinction.
The Lemurs and Snow Leopards are just two of the many animals the Melbourne Zoo hosts and works to conserve.
Others include the African Wild Dog, Asian Elephant, Sumatran Tiger, and Tasmanian Devil.
