Meet Asha, Manju and Sikari!

The cubs were born on January 26, 2020!
Miska and Kang-Ju are their mom and dad. All the cubs were born healthy.


Two of the cubs were girls, Asha and Manju and one was a boy named Sikari.


The cubs will stay with their mom in a private den to grow and bond for a few months.

When they are ready, they will move into a larger enclosure.





The cubs are very playful and are growing fast!
